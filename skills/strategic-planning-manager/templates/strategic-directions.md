## What Makes a Strategic Direction

Strategic directions are NOT just goals. They are:

- **Innovative Actions** - New approaches, not business as usual
- **Contradiction Resolvers** - Address the tensions identified
- **Vision Enablers** - Create conditions for vision achievement
- **Multi-Year Commitments** - Sustained effort over the plan period
