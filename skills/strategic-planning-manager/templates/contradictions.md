# Underlying Contradictions

> Tensions that must be resolved—not just barriers to remove—for the vision to be achieved.

---

## Understanding Contradictions

**Key Distinction:**
- **Barriers** = Obstacles to remove or overcome
- **Contradictions** = Tensions between competing priorities or values that require resolution

Contradictions often take the form: "We need X, but Y pulls us the opposite direction."
